A Brief Guide to Commercial Property Insurance

Are you looking for ways to save your company in the event of an emergency? Do you need a way to protect your assets, such as the commercial property you own? If so, then you need to learn all that you can about commercial property insurance.

Doing so will help you understand the benefits of it, how it can help you, and what you should look for in a commercial business insurance policy. This can be the saving grace for your company in the event of an unforeseen occurrence.

See below for an in-depth guide on commercial property insurance, as well as everything you need to know on the subject.

Not sure if you need startup insurance in Massachusetts? Keep reading and discover if you should get it.

What is Commercial Property Insurance?

Let's begin by understanding what commercial property insurance is and why it's so important that you invest in a policy.

As with many small businesses, your company has gained certain assets along the way. Certain things are crucial to running your business efficiently and making sure the operations are in optimal condition.

You've used your profits and earnings to gain these assets, but it's unlikely that you have the cash to recover them if they were to be damaged or stolen.

That's where commercial property insurance comes into play. As the name would imply, these policies cover any sort of commercial property that your business has accrued.

that way, your business's future isn't stalled or dampened by an unforeseen emergency, such as a fire, natural disaster, vandalism, or theft. When you file a claim, your provider will make sure that everything is covered, repaired, or replaced.

It's important to find the right provider for your commercial property insurance needs. If you go with a lesser provider, then they might be more selective on what they define to be "assets", leading to items that aren't covered when an emergency hits.

What Are the Benefits of Commercial Property Insurance?

As you've probably noticed from this article already, there are numerous benefits to investing in commercial property insurance for your brand. See below for a bit more information.

1. Coverage for Wide Range of Events

Unfortunately, there is a wide range of emergencies that threaten the day-to-day operations (and profits) of your company.

For that reason, you need to invest in insurance policies that can cover the damage in case any of those emergencies were to ever arise, such as commercial property insurance.

One of the main benefits of business property insurance is the vast amount of emergencies that it covers, such as:

  • Break-ins to your commercial building
  • Damage during indemnity periods
  • Damage from any form of natural disaster (tornado, hurricane, thunderstorms, etc.)
  • Tenant lawsuits (if applicable)
  • Injuries that occur on your property (EX: customer slips and falls on your floor)

2. Ability to Save Money

As with most insurance policies, commercial property insurance (with the right provider) offers you a bunch of ways to save on money.

This helps you find a policy that gives you the perfect amount of coverage, while still finding reasonable rates that fit in with your company budget.

With Econosurance, we use several factors to get you as low of premiums as possible, such as:

  • Offering competitive rates
  • Combining your commercial property insurance policy with others that we provide
  • Helping you understand tax implications
  • Looking into the value of the building
  • Performing due diligence on the neighborhood of the business

The list goes on and on. We're out to give you the coverage you need without having to pay an unnecessary amount to get your hands on it.

3. Protection for Your Assets

You and your staff are working towards a bigger and brighter future for your business each day. Don't let an unforeseen event cause all of that to come crashing to the ground.

Commercial property insurance offers you the protection that you need. In the event of an emergency, it will be a minor setback until you get back on your feet.

In time, you'll have all of the assets you need once more, thus turning your operations back into a well-oiled machine!

What to Look for in a Provider

Now that you have seen what commercial property insurance is and the benefits that it can offer your company, you can start to look for a policy.

Before that, you need to make sure you invest in a policy with a trusted provider. With so many providers out there, how can you make sure you're hiring one you can trust?

Here are a few qualities you should look for in an insurance provider:

  • Multiple Forms of Coverage-- The more types of policies that a provider can offer, the more policies that you can bundle to save on premium costs.
  • Transparency-- You want someone that genuinely cares about your business. When you talk to a provider, make sure they offer upfront and honest feedback.
  • Positive Reputation-- Let the provider's previous and current clientele tell the story. Look at reviews online, ask for client references, and read through customer testimonials.
  • Customer Service-- When you ask questions, do they take the time to answer? Do the online reviews have positive things to say about the provider's customer support?
