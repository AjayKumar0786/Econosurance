What We Do

We have over 25 years of proven success as an independent insurance provider in Massachusetts, helping individuals, families, and businesses protect their assets and develop financial security. We carefully consider all the information our clients share with us and then compile the right policy for their needs at the best possible price – whether it is auto insurance, homeowner’s insurance, business owner’s insurance, or any other policy that helps our clients.

Our Mission and Values

We know your time is extremely precious. That is why we have made it our mission to offer reliable and convenient service that puts our clients’ needs first and saves them both money and time. We value continuous learning and strive to stay on top of industry trends.

Independent Agency

Unlike branded agencies who only sell you their insurance, whether it’s the perfect fit for your needs or not, Econosurance is an independent agent, indebted to no company and completely free to find you the insurance policies with the best coverage at the most affordable rates.

Loyalty to Customers

We want each and every one of our clients to be happy and satisfied with their insurance. Our loyalty is to our clients and we work hard to maintain our reputation. We hope that with our help, our clients find peace of mind to focus on enjoying all the good that life has to offer.

Commitment to Quality

We are perfectly positioned within the insurance market to provide anyone with the coverage that they need. You can be assured that we will go above and beyond, including – but not limited to – the following:

● Regular Check-ins — We keep track of changes to our client's situation to ensure their insurance coverage and cost are in accordance with their needs.

● Top-Quality Partners — We partner with companies we personally trust and believe in. We are proud to work with insurance carriers who receive excellent ratings from third-party rating agencies and whose services are valued by millions of people.
