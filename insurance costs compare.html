How do YOUR insurance costs compare?

Econosurance ranks as one of the best insurance agencies because our loyalty is to our clients. We shop our clients’ policies at inception and at renewals to ensure they are absolutely paying the lowest insurance rate for the most coverage.

Factors affecting your auto insurance rates

The price you pay for your insurance may vary by hundreds of dollars depending upon what you are insuring and with whom.

Here are six common factors that determine your insurance rate:

Garaging Town

The ValuePenguin study suggests that the town where you live makes a big difference in your insurance rate. While actuaries are responsible for financial consequences, we don’t know all the elements that go into mitigating risk from one town to another. For instance, some have asked, “What if my vehicle is parked in a garage?” Unfortunately, in most instances where you park is irrelevant..

Experience

Unlike states that determine experience based on driver age, Massachusetts considers experience based upon the actual number of years licensed. It doesn’t matter if you were first licensed in another country, if you can prove licensure most carriers will accept it. Otherwise, you pay a higher premium if you have less than three years of driving experience and/or more than three years but less than six years.

Driving History

Incurring points on your driving record costs you dearly, no doubt about it. Everyone starts with a score of 00. From there, you can go up or down to 99 or 98. Anything that’s not a 99, 98, or 00 incurs a surcharge which has a tremendous impact on rate and acceptability to carriers. ”At fault” accidents are also heavily penalized. Even not at fault, frequent glass claims, and hit-and-run incidents are heavily weighted against the insured when it comes to premiums.

Coverage

Insurance coverage is like buying a car, the more options you add the more costly it becomes.

Vehicle

You can’t expect to pay the same for a Maserati as you do for a Ford. If it’s a luxury vehicle, you will pay more, even if it’s an older model.

Carrier

Rates differ from carrier to carrier. Sometimes it’s a slight difference and sometimes it can be a significant one. Carriers adjust their rates at the minimum every year, therefore, it’s likely you’ll see a rate increase yearly or semi-annually.

Here are ways you can save on car insurance

From shopping your policies around with our carriers to finding you the most affordable coverage to suit your needs, Econosurance has you covered. Below are some ways that have proven to save our clients money while meticulously protecting their assets.

Higher deductible

A deductible is the dollar amount out of pocket that you are responsible for paying toward a covered loss. The higher your deductible, the more money you are likely to save on your insurance premium. For example, with a $1,000 deductible, you may reduce your insurance rate by $500 or more.

Bundle Homeowners and Auto Policies

Many insurers who sell homeowners insurance also offer a bundling discount. If you buy two or more insurance policies from the same carrier, you may be eligible to receive a discount. To ensure you’re getting the best price, agents at Econosurance make certain any combined price from one insurer is lower than buying the coverages separately from different companies.

Shop

Having insurance on everything you own is undoubtedly expensive—but it is also your protection against potential disaster and financial losses. Insurance rates vary from company to company, so let us do the shopping for you and get you the best deal available. Learn more.

Other ways to save

  • Consider insurance rates in the area where you plan to buy or rent a home.
  • Driving history is important. If you can avoid moving violations and accidents, you will save yourself a lot of money on auto insurance and carriers will fight for your business. It’s a good situation to be in.
  • You have leverage on coverage; you can save a bundle by increasing your deductible to buy more coverage.
  • You always have the option to buy a cheaper car, but if you opt for a luxury vehicle, be prepared to pay a premium for insurance.
  • Unless you are a delinquent driver, you can find great savings shopping for carriers. You can also decide to switch at any time. In auto insurance as in life, there are consequences for overstaying your welcome.
